The State of the World Today!

What do you love about the world?

I love the interconnectedness of the world. I love the way that everything is right at our fingertips. Anything we would like to know we can just look up on the internet. I love how we have an accumalative pool of knowledge.

What do you hate about the world?

I hate that there is so much violence. I hate that people spend more time texting than having actual conversations.

What do you wish you could change about the world?

I wish I could change the life expectancy of Planet Earth. I would want it to last forever.
